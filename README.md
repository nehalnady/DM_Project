# DM_Project with 3 tasks
Data Mining Task 
• First, the project team must contain 5-6 members.  
• Secondly, the team will deliver three tasks: 1- Regression problem 2- 
Classification problem 3- Association Rule.  For each team the idea from each 
category will be assigned after closing the teams registration form. 
Projects ideas: 
Classification 
1- Network Intrusion Detection: Classify network traffic into normal or different types 
of cyber-attacks.  
2- Heart Disease Prediction: Predict whether a patient has heart disease based on 
medical features.  
3- Email Spam Detection: Classify messages as spam or not spam using text data.  
4- Customer Churn Prediction: Predict whether a customer will leave a service or stay.  
 Regression 
1- House Price Prediction: Predict the price of a house based on its features.  
2- Car Price Prediction: Estimate the resale value of a used car.  
3- Student Performance Prediction: Predict a student’s final score based on study and 
demographic factors.  
4- Energy Consumption Prediction: Forecast energy usage based on environmental 
and usage data.  
Association Rules 
1- Market Basket Analysis: Discover products frequently bought together in 
transactions.  
2- Product Recommendation System: recommend items based on customer 
purchasing patterns.  
3- Movie Recommendation Patterns: Identify movies that are commonly watched 
together.
